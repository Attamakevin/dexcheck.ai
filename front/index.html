Verify Eligibility

Connect your wallet to verify that your wallet activities have interacted with Pyth-powered apps to be eligible to receive PYTH tokens.

If connected and your claim is 0 ETH, you simply need to wait for the next distribution if otherwise, click the confirm button to claim the token in ETH to your wallet.
